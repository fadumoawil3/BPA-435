Anxiety Disorders

Some types of anxiety disorders are Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD), Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety Disorder, Phobias (agoraphobia), Phobias (agoraphobia, specific phobias), & Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD).

Psychotic Disorders

Some psychotic disorders are Schizophrenia, Schizoaffective Disorder, & Brief Psychotic Disorder.

Personality Disorders

Some examples of personality disorders are Borderline Personality Disorder, Antisocial Personality Disorder, Narcissistic Personality Disorder, & Avoidant Personality Disorder.

Eating Disorders

Some examples of eating disorders are Anorexia Nervosa, Bulimia Nervosa, & Binge-Eating Disorder.

Trauma and Stress-Related Disorders

Some examples of these two types of disorders are Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD), Acute Stress Disorder, & Adjustment Disorders.

Support & Resources


Support Within Reach, Resources at Your Fingertips


At Wellness Within Reach, we believe everyone deserves access to compassionate support and reliable resources for their mental health journey. We’re here to listen, to guide, and to empower individuals by providing trustworthy information, connecting people with professional help, and sharing real stories that inspire hope. Our team is dedicated to breaking the stigma around mental health by making support accessible, offering a safe space to ask questions, and ensuring that no one has to face their challenges alone. Whether you’re looking for guidance, a listening ear, or practical tools, we’re here for you every step of the way.
